Internship Profile: Nick Fogarty at PlayLab, Inc.

12/10/2015. Contributed by Nick Fogarty.

PlayLab, Inc. is an arts office located in the West Village in New York City. Right now, they're working on an app that makes reality-tv watching competitive and a floating water-filtering pool in the East River, called +POOL.

At the internship, I had the opportunity to work on a few of their own studio explorations and client projects.

I spent most of my summer at PlayLab working on the concept and identity for LonelyChats Today, a chat line and companionship service. Here, I designed the garments for the A/W Infinity Collection and directed the photography for the launch set for later this season.

Nick Fogarty is a senior graphic design major.
